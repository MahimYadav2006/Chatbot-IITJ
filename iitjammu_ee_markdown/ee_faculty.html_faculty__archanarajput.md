Cloaking/Invisbility, Frequency Selective Surfaces, Transformation Optics, Antennas, Metamaterials

**Teaching Interests**

Electromagnetics, Network Analysis And Synthesis, Basic Electronics, Microwave Engineering, Antenna Theory And Design

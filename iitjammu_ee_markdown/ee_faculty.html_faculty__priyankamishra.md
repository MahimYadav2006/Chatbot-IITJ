#### Priyanka Mishra

Priyanka Mishra completed her Bachelor’s in Electrical Engineering from Galgotia’s College of Engineering and Technology, India in 2013. She received her M. Tech and Ph. D. degrees in Energy Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, India in 2017 and 2022, respectively. Her Masters’ work won as best post award by Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, India. Her Ph. D. work is awarded by POSOCO, India in doctoral category as one of the best Ph. D. Thesis in Power Systems. She worked as a Postdoctoral Associate at State University of New York at Stony Brook, New York, USA from April, 2022 to October, 2023. Key milestones during this research were development of provably resilient, programmable networked microgrids. She worked as HVDC Control and Protection Engineer at GE Renewables, Noida, India from November 2023 to January 2026. Out of her research, she published 7 journals and 5 conference papers, including one book chapter. She received outstanding reviewer award in IEEE Journal of Ocean Engineering in 2022. Her research interests include solar photovoltaic power generation and power system protection integrating inverter-based renewable energy sources.

##### Research Interest

Protection of microgrid integrating renewable energy sources (solar power, wind power, etc); Neural active fault management control of power-electronic converter-based renewable energy sources; Time-domain relaying; Converter transformer protection.

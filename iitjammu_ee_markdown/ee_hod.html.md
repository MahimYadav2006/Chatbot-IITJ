## Dr. Ravikant Saini

#### Message

Welcome to the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian
Institute of Technology Jammu. In line with the Institute's focus on academics-oriented
research, the department considers both teaching and research integral. The department
accommodates highly experienced and dedicated faculty members who are great teachers and
researchers in their respective domains. We are also supported by invaluable staff
members who discharge their duties efficiently and enthusiastically on the technical and
administrative front. We look for zealous young minds for our UG, PG, and PhD programs,
as they are our greatest strength.

Our department and the Institute's
instrumental/computational facility are well-equipped to execute research on various
fields of Electrical Engineering. Contemporarily and fundamentally, the overall growth
of our department and, consequently, of the Institute and society at large drives us.
Each member at all levels actively and innovatively contributes to reaching the utmost
development of our department. We welcome you to be a part of our department and our
endeavours.

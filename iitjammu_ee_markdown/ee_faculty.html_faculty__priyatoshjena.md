#### Priyatosh Jena

I am Dr. Priyatosh Jena, currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jammu. I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University), Varanasi, India, my M.Tech. degree in Electrical Engineering with specialization in Renewable Energy and Energy Management from the National Institute of Technology Arunachal Pradesh, India, and my B.Tech. degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the International Institute of Information Technology, Bhubaneswar, India. I am a member of the IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS) Educational Videos Committee. I was awarded the Chairman’s Gold Medal and the Institute Gold Medal from NIT Arunachal Pradesh in recognition of outstanding academic performance in the M.Tech program. I am also a recipient of the 14th GRID-INDIA Power System Awards (GIPSA) 2025–26.

##### Research Interest

Battery Charging System, Bidirectional Converter, Reconfigurable Power Converter, and Soft-Switched Converter.

##### Awards & honours

**A1. GRID-INDIA Power System Award (GIPSA) – Ph.D. Category (2025–26)**

Awarded to **Priyatosh Jena** for outstanding doctoral research titled *“Soft-Switching Modulation Techniques for Bidirectional Dual Active Bridge Converter Based Battery Charging Systems.”* The award is conferred under the CSR initiative of Grid Controller of India Limited (GRID-INDIA), formerly Power System Operation Corporation Limited (POSOCO), recognizing exemplary Ph.D. research contributions in power systems and allied domains.

**A2. Best Thesis Award – IEEE STPEC 2025, Goa**

**Priyatosh Jena** received the **Best Ph.D. Thesis Award** at the 2025 4th IEEE International Conference on Smart Technologies for Power, Energy and Control (STPEC) 2025 held in Goa, India, for the doctoral thesis titled *“Soft-Switching Modulation Techniques for Bidirectional Dual Active Bridge Converter Based Battery Charging Systems.”*

**A3. Chairman’s Gold Medal (2018)**

Awarded to **Priyatosh Jena** for securing the **highest CGPA among all students of the Electrical Engineering branch** in the postgraduate program (2016–2018) at National Institute of Technology Arunachal Pradesh. The medal was presented during the **5th Convocation** by the Chief Guest Sanjay Hemant Inamdar.

**A4. Institute Gold Medal (2018)**

Awarded to **Priyatosh Jena** for achieving the **highest CGPA in the Renewable Energy and Energy Management M.Tech. program** offered by the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Institute of Technology Arunachal Pradesh. The medal was presented during the **5th Convocation** by Anil Dattatraya Sahasrabudhe, Chairman of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

#### Underwater Research Lab

#### [Underwater Research lab](https://iitjammu.ac.in/ee/)

At IIT Jammu a unique and first kind of research platform is developed
with a size of 25ft x 20ft which is having similar facility of ocean including the
waves and artificial noise injection process in ocean.

## From The Desk of HoD

![Image](https://iitjammu.ac.in/ee/assets/images/electrical/Ravikant Saini.jpg)

Welcome to the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of
Technology Jammu. In line with the Institute's focus on
academics-oriented research, the department considers both teaching and
research integral. The department accommodates highly experienced and
dedicated faculty members who are great teachers and researchers in
their respective domains. We are also supported by invaluable staff
members who discharge their duties efficiently and enthusiastically on
the technical and administrative front. We look for zealous young minds
for our UG, PG, and PhD programs, as they are our greatest strength.

Dr. Ravikant Saini
Head of Department

#### Arun Kumar Verma

Dr. Verma earned his master’s and Ph.D. from I.I.T. Delhi, New Delhi, India. He served as a visiting professor at Ontario Tech University, Oshawa, Ontario, Canada, in 2024. He was a Postdoctoral research fellow (PDF) at the Energy Research Institute (ERI@N), Nanyang Technological University (NTU) Singapore, from 2015 to 2016. Before joining NTU Singapore, he worked as a visiting graduate researcher (VGR) at the Smart Grid Energy Research Center (SMERC), University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), California, USA, from 2014 to 2015. He won the prestigious BASE fellowship for advanced solar energy research in 2014. He has been conferred with the prestigious POSOCO Power System Research Award 2016. He was also selected for the SERB International Research Experience (SIRE) award in 2023. He is an associate Editor for IEEE Transactions on Transportation Electrification and an associate editor for IEEE Journal of Emerging and Selected Topics in Industrial Electronics, as well as an assistant editor of the Smart Grid journal and a member of the editorial board for the EPE Journal. He has been invited by Defence University College, Ethiopia, and Metal Engineering Corporation, Ethiopia, as an instructor for the M.Tech program in Renewable Energy Engineering from 7 May 2016 to 27 May 2016. To name a few. He has/completed DST research funding of 7.5 cr., including Mission Innovation (MI), SPARC, ECR, SERD, and CRG. He is also the mentor director of a startup, Rishi Agastaya Technologies Pvt. Ltd. Dr Verma has guided 10 PhD and 18 M.Tech theses. Six PhD candidates are currently working under his supervision.

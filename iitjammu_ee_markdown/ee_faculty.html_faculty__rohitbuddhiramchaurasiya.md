#### Rohit Buddhiram Chaurasiya

##### Research Interest

1. VLSI Design (Includes: Hardware-Friendly Algorithm Design, Digital VLSI Architecture Design, ASIC Chip Design and its Testing, FPGA Prototyping) of Stand-Alone, Cooperative and Wideband Spectrum Sensors for Cognitive Radio Network.
2. Low-Power and Hardware-Efficient Implementation of Post-Quantum Cryptography.

##### Education Qualification

1. Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics Engineering from University of Mumbai.(2008-2012).
2. Master of Engineering in Electronics Engineering from University of Mumbai.(2012-2014).
3. Ph.D. (Thesis Title: Hardware-Efficient VLSI-Algorithms and Digital-Architectures of Standalone and Cooperative Spectrum Sensors for Cognitive Radio Network) from IIT Mandi. (2017-2022)

##### Academic Interests

1. Digital Electronics
2. Basic VLSI Design
3. FPGA Based System Design
4. System Design using VHDL and Verilog
5. Digital IC Design
6. Digital VLSI Architecture Design
7. ASIC Chip Design
8. CMOS Analog IC Design
9. Advanced Digital System Design
10. Electronic Devices and Circuits

##### Research Experience

1. Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics Engineering at Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology, Navi Mumbai (Presently: Dr. D. Y. Patil, Deemed To Be University, Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology, Navi Mumbai). {2015-2017}
2. Assistant Professor (On Contract) in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Naya Raipur, Plot No. 7, near Purkhoti Muktangan, Sector 24, Atal Nagar-Nava Raipur, Uparwara, Chhattisgarh 493661. {2022-2023}

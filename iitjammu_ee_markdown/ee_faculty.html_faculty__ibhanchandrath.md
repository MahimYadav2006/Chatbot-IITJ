#### Ibhanchand Rath

Ibhan Chand Rath received the B.Tech. degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the International Institute of Information Technology, Bhubaneswar, India, in 2014, and the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay, India, in 2024. He worked as a System Integration and Testing Consultant at Igrenenergi Services Private Limited, Research Park, IIT BOMBAY, from May 2024 to December 2025. Currently, he is serving as Assistant Professor in the Electrical Engineering Department of IIT JAMMU from January 2026.

##### Research Interest

Grid connected hybrid multivelvel converter topology design, Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTs), High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC), Grid integration of Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS), Battery Management System (BMS), V2G and G2V EV charging applications,

##### Academic Interests

Power Electronics, power systems, electric machines, and control systems

##### Research Experience

**Doctoral Research Experience**

Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay

Duration: 2015 – 2024

Areas of Research:

i. Development of hybrid multilevel power converter topologies for FACTS and HVDC applications.

ii. Power quality improvement of three phase AC grids, including reactive power compensation, unbalance mitigation, and harmonic compensation.

iii. Control algorithm development for capacitor voltage regulation in multilevel converters under unbalanced three-phase conditions.

iv. Application of multilevel converters for simultaneous charging of multiple electric vehicles (EVs).

**Industry Research Experience**

IgrenEnergi Services Private Limited

Duration: 2024 – 2025

Area of Work: Development of Battery Management Systems (BMS) for two- and four-wheeler electric vehicles, including passive and active cell balancing of battery packs

#### Sudhakar Modem

##### Education Qualification

- Ph.D.**,**Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering Department,(Submitted on 29/09/17; Defended on 9/2/18)

- Thesis: Optimization of Networks with Energy Harvesting Relays.

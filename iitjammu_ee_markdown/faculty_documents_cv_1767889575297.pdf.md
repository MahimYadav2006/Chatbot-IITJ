Brief
Dr. Arun Kumar Verma earned his master’s and Ph.D. from I.I.T. Delhi, New Delhi, India. He served as a visiting professor at Ontario Tech
University, Oshawa, Ontario, Canada, in 2024. He was a post-doctoral research fellow (PDF) at the Energy Research Institute (ERI@N),
Nanyang Technological University (NTU) Singapore, from 2015 to 2016. Before joining NTU Singapore, he worked as a visiting graduate
researcher (VGR) at the Smart Grid Energy Research Center (SMERC), University of California Los Angeles (UCLA), California, USA, from
2014-2015. He won the prestigious BASE fellowship for advanced solar energy research in 2014. He has been conferred with the prestigious
POSOCO Power System Research Award 2016. He also won the SERB SIRE fellowship. He is an associate editor for IEEE Transactions on
Transportation Electrification and also Associate editor for IEEE Emerging and Selected Topics in Indistrial Electronics.. He is a guest editor in
the IET Power Electronics special issue, an assistant editor of the smart grid journal, and a member of the editorial board for EPE Journal. His
research interests include power electronics, converters for renewable energy and EV chargers, bi-directional power flow, and electric
transportation.

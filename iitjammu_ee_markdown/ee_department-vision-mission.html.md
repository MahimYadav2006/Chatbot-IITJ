### Vision of the Department

- To ensure excellence across a wide range of disciplines, professions, and arts
while encouraging investigation across disciplinary boundaries with creative
learning.
- To nurture holistic engineers to address global challenges through research,
innovation, inspiration, and influence.
- To educate the next generation of engineering leaders, create new knowledge,
serve society, and transform our society for the better.
- To provide students with a foundational understanding of a broad range of
disciplines followed by opportunities for in-depth study with hands-on in their
chosen fields.
- To advance impactful knowledge and practice.
- To attract talented professionals and academicians of the world: to create,
innovate, and discover the unknown.

### Mission of the Department

- To educate the next generation of engineering leaders, to create new knowledge,
and to serve the society and to transform our society for the better.
- To provide students with a foundational understanding of a broad range of
disciplines followed by the opportunity for in-depth study with hands-on
experience in a chosen discipline.
- Discovering and advancing knowledge and practice.
- To attract the most talented people in the world: to create, to innovate, and to
see the unseen

#### Shikha Baghel

I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jammu (IIT Jammu). Prior to this, I held faculty positions as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Tirupati and in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), Surathkal. Before transitioning to academia, I worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher in the LEAP Lab at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore. I earned my Ph.D. in Signal Processing from the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati). My research interests lie in the areas of speech and audio processing, speaker and language diarization, dialect and language identification, as well as the application of signal processing, machine learning, and deep learning techniques.

##### Research Interest

Speech and Audio Processing, Speaker Diarization, Language Diarization, Dialect/Language Identification, Applications of signal processing, Applications of Machine Learning and Deep Learning.
